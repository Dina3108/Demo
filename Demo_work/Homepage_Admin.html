KPRIET Hostel Community is a website designed for the hostel students of KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology (KPRIET), accommodating 600 boys, 300 girls, 400 international students, and 100 working staff members residing in hostels including Thiruvalluvar, Cheran, Bharathi, Pandiyan, and Pallavan for men, and Ganga, Yamuna, and Kaveri for women. Developed by the AURA Club of KPRIET, the website offers useful features tailored to the needs of hostel students
